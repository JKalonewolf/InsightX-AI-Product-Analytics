# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy & Stakeholder Presentation
## Project: InsightX – AI Product Analytics Platform

### 1. Go-To-Market Launch Plan

#### **Phase 1: Developer Beta (Months 1-2)**
*   **Target**: 100 early-stage SaaS developers and product-centric engineers.
*   **Action**: Offer free access to the "Pro Plan" for 6 months in exchange for detailed product feedback and bug reports.
*   **Metric**: Goal is to capture 100 million total ingestion events to test queue latency.

#### **Phase 2: Product Hunt & Launch (Month 3)**
*   **Target**: Mid-market SaaS founders, Product Managers, and Growth Teams.
*   **Action**: Execute a launch campaign on Product Hunt, Hacker News, and LinkedIn.
*   **Offer**: 20% lifetime discount for early bird registrants.

#### **Phase 3: Outbound Enterprise Push (Months 4-6)**
*   **Target**: Series-A+ tech companies with dedicated product teams.
*   **Action**: Account-Based Marketing (ABM) emails showcasing the cost savings of moving from Amplitude ($1k+/mo) to InsightX ($199/mo) and highlighting the native ML predictions.

---

### 2. Marketing Channels
1.  **Developer Relations**: Direct community engagement on Reddit (r/webdev, r/saas), Dev.to, and GitHub.
2.  **SEO & Content Marketing**: Articles comparing analytics approaches (e.g. "Why simple SQL queries fail for cohort retention analysis").
3.  **Pricing Competitiveness**: High-visibility transparent pricing tables to draw users away from Amplitude's hidden contact-sales price model.

---

### 3. Stakeholder Presentation Slides

---
#### **[Slide 1] Project Overview & Opportunity**
*   **Headline**: Product Analytics is Broken.
*   **Context**:
    *   *Google Analytics 4 (GA4)* is designed for marketers, not product teams.
    *   *Amplitude & Mixpanel* are powerful but lock essential analytics (cohorts, A/B statistics) behind expensive enterprise gates.
    *   Product teams lack automated diagnostic engines, leading to wasted engineering hours writing custom SQL to solve conversion drops.

---
#### **[Slide 2] The Solution: InsightX**
*   **One Integration**: Add one line of JavaScript to start tracking.
*   **Descriptive Analytics**: Instant access to funnel pipelines and retention cohorts.
*   **Predictive Diagnostics**: Built-in machine learning models that calculate churn, project future MRR, and list feature recommendations out-of-the-box.

---
#### **[Slide 3] The Revenue & SaaS Business Model**
*   **Developer Free Tier**: 100,000 monthly events, core dashboard, 1 project.
*   **Growth Pro Tier ($99/mo)**: 5 million monthly events, funnel analyzers, A/B significance calculators, core AI insights.
*   **Enterprise Tier ($499/mo)**: Custom event allocations, advanced time-series churn predictive classifiers, and dedicated database instances.

---
#### **[Slide 4] Current Traction & Roadmap**
*   *Beta launch completed.* Full SQLite schema, JS Client SDK, FastAPI ingestion receiver, and analytical reporting dashboard are currently operational.
*   *Q3 Target*: Finalize the predictive ML models and expand integration channels to Slack and Discord.
